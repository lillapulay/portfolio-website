About me


Hi, my name is Lilla. 👋🏻

Last year I relocated to the Netherlands and not long after decided to pursue a more fitting career path. I realized that I wanted to acquire both technical skills and an opportunity to work on creative tasks.

I have long been interested in Web Development due to its unique combination of creative and technical requirements, so this year I took the leap and enrolled in an online course. I am currently studying in the CareerFoundry Full-Stack Web Development course.

Since I'm also interested in User Experience, I'm keeping an eye out for any knowledge I can pick up in this field and will start more in-depth studies upon finishing my current studies. I aspire to become a Front-End Developer with a strong knowledge of UX and combine these fields to create high-quality products.

As a former Jurist transitioning into the world of Web Development, I bring an eye for detail, excellent analytical skills and the ability to keep up with a fast-paced environment.

In my new field I aim to utilize problem solving skills and proactivity in any creative process that comes with building web applications. With an understanding of the human nature, I visualize the outcome of important features about to be implemented and keep principles like usability and accessibility in mind.

I am looking forward to becoming part of a user-centered team at a company with a growth mindset, and I hope to combine technical knowledge and creative skills in my daily work, either on-site or remotely.

Apart from Development world, I'm fond of nature and traveling. I enjoy gaming, studying languages, reading, music and consider myself a life-long learner.
