Front-end Development

I started studying Web Development in the beginning of 2020 and the client-side is definitely where I feel at home the most. During this time I gained experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and several libraries/frameworks (e.g. jQuery, React, Angular, Vue). I enjoy working on well-structured, eye-catching interfaces that are practical and easy to use, and make sure the layout is responsive and the application works well in different browsers.

User Experience (UX)

The importance of prioritizing the user's needs and comfort is unquestionable. After having met with the topic of User Experience at a company event some 2 years ago, I had the opportunity to attend a short course on it and I have been fond of this field ever since. Because UX goes hand in hand with Web Development, I make sure to educate myself on core UX principles and implement them in my development projects.
